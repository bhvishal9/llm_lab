## Observations
- Factual and multi_hop retrieval is strong at this threshold
- Out-of-scope abstention is the primary failure mode — threshold tuning or a dedicated "no answer" mechanism needed
- The two factual misses are both from the gazetteer, both obscure topics — worth watching in future runs to see if they are consistent failures
- Multi-hop 5/5 is encouraging but the queries were designed around heavily indexed topics; harder multi-hop queries may reveal gaps
